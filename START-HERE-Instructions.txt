💐 WELCOME TO YOUR EVER AFTER WEDDING PLANNER!
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

Congratulations on your engagement! Let's get your
planner set up. It takes 5 minutes — no tech
skills needed! We'll walk through it together. 💕

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 1: UNZIP THE FOLDER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Don't worry, this is easier than it sounds!

  • Find the file you downloaded:
    "Ever-After-Planner.zip"
    (usually in your Downloads folder)

  • RIGHT-CLICK on it
    → Choose "Extract All" (Windows)
    → OR just double-click (Mac)

  • A new folder will appear right next to the
    zip file. That's your planner! 📁

  You can delete the zip file now if you'd like —
  you don't need it anymore.


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 2: OPEN YOUR PLANNER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

  • Open the new folder

  • You'll see three files:
    ✦ index.html
    ✦ style.css
    ✦ script.js

  • DOUBLE-CLICK on "index.html"
    (this is your planner!)

  • Your planner opens in your web browser ✨


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 3: BOOKMARK IT (so you don't lose it!)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

  • With the planner open in your browser, press:
    → Ctrl + D (Windows)
    → Cmd + D (Mac)

  • Name it "My Wedding Planner"
  • Click "Save"

  Your planner is now in your bookmarks forever! 🔖


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 4: START PLANNING!
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

  • Click any tab at the top to explore:
    Overview, Wedding Day, Appointments, Budget,
    Guests, Seating, Venue, Events, Wedding Party,
    Gifts, Mood Board, Honeymoon

  • Fill in your details on "Overview" first

  • Everything saves AUTOMATICALLY as you type!
    No save button needed. 💾


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
💡 HOW YOUR DATA WORKS (the friendly truth!)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Think of your planner like a notebook. When you
  write in it, the words stay on those pages.

  Your planner saves your information IN THE
  BROWSER you open it in. That's it! Simple!

  ✨ THE GOOD NEWS:
     Your data stays safe even when you close
     the browser or shut down your computer.
     Come back tomorrow — it's all still there!

  🌿 TWO THINGS TO KNOW:

  1) If you open your planner in a DIFFERENT
     BROWSER (like switching from Chrome to
     Safari), it will look empty — but don't
     panic! Your original data is still safe
     in the first browser. Just go back to it,
     or follow Step 5 below to bring your data
     with you.

  2) If you open your planner on a DIFFERENT
     DEVICE (like your phone instead of your
     laptop), same thing — it will start empty
     on that device. Your data isn't lost!
     It's just sitting in the first place you
     used.

  💡 THE EASIEST RULE:
     Pick ONE browser on ONE device as your
     "wedding planning home." That's where
     everything lives. You can always bring
     a copy somewhere else — see Step 5!


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 5: BACK UP YOUR DATA (do this weekly!)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

  At the top of your planner, you'll see two
  buttons: "Export Data" and "Import"

  📥 EXPORT DATA:
     Click this once a week to download a backup
     file. It'll be called something like
     "wedding-planner-backup-2026-04-17.json"

     Save it somewhere safe! Some ideas:
       • Email it to yourself
       • Save to Google Drive / iCloud / Dropbox
       • Put it in a "Wedding" folder

  📤 IMPORT:
     If you ever need to restore your data
     (new computer, new browser, accidental
     clearing of browsing history), click
     Import and choose your backup file —
     everything comes back! 🎉

  That's your safety net. Easy peasy!

❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: My planner won't open!
A: Make sure you UNZIPPED the folder first.
   You need to see the regular folder with
   three files inside — not the zip file
   itself.

Q: I closed my browser. Is my data gone?
A: Nope, it's still there! Open your bookmark
   (or double-click index.html again) and
   everything is waiting for you. Promise! 💕

Q: Can I use this on my phone?
A: Yes! Email the folder to yourself, or save
   it to Google Drive / iCloud, then open
   index.html from your phone. Just remember
   it'll be a separate copy from your laptop —
   use Export/Import to sync between them.

Q: Can my partner see it too?
A: Each device keeps its own copy. The easiest
   way to share: use Export to download a
   backup, email it to your partner, and they
   use Import on their own copy. Or just plan
   together on one device!

Q: What happens if I clear my browser history?
A: Your planner data might get wiped along
   with cookies. That's why we recommend
   exporting a backup once a week — you can
   always restore it with Import!

Q: Something looks funny or broken!
A: Try refreshing the page (press F5 or
   Ctrl+R). If that doesn't fix it, message me
   on Etsy — I'm happy to help! 💌
